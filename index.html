Our Story

Ultra East was built in the East Rand with one mission: to design the engines that the next generation of youth culture will run on.

Starting from grassroots community events and talent development, Ultra East evolved into a Hybrid Operations Company with a multi-dimensional ecosystem of IP, platforms, partnerships, and youth-focused experiences.
